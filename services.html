Services

Root Canal and Teeth Whitening

At denTallo, we offer both advanced root canal treatments and professional teeth whitening to restore your oral health and enhance your smile

Orthodontics and Dental Filling

Provide comprehensive orthodontic treatments to straighten teeth and correct bite issues, along with durable dental fillings to restore damaged teeth. Our orthodontic solutions include braces and aligners for all ages, while our tooth-colored fillings blend seamlessly with your natural teeth for a beautiful, healthy smile.

Tooth Extraction Treatment

We perform gentle and safe tooth extractions when a tooth cannot be saved or needs to be removed. Our experienced team uses advanced techniques and local anesthesia to ensure minimal discomfort. We handle simple extractions, surgical extractions, and wisdom tooth removal with care, always prioritizing your comfort and providing clear aftercare instructions for optimal healing.

Daily Routine Check-up

Regular dental check-ups are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. At denTallo, our comprehensive routine examinations include thorough teeth and gum assessment, professional cleaning, oral cancer screening, and X-rays when needed. We also provide personalized oral hygiene advice and preventive care recommendations to help you maintain a healthy smile between visits.
